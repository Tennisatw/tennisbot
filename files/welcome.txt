Welcome to Tennisbot
This is a bot assistant based on GPT-4 and the Discord API, with a variety of functions, such as setting alarms, querying the weather, and simply handle complex questions. It can be used on Discord.
If you are using it for the first time, please open files/bot_config.ini, set your Discord and ChatGPT tokens in the TOKEN column, as well as the language in the DEFAULT column.
For any questions, you can directly ask Tennisbot, or contact the developer Tennisatw: tennisatw@mail.com
